The 5 Cutest Valentine’s Day Cards This Side of Equestria

1. Donut Sleep on This One

Kawaii smiles, hand-holding, hundreds and thousands, pun-based humour. This card couldn’t get any sweeter if it was dipped in frosting and dusted with icing sugar. And if donuts are a little sugary for your tastes, check out the rest of the cutie-pie puns at the Magically Crafted Etsy shop (You’re My Cup Of Tea is another strong favourite). £2.80 shipped to the UK, £4.30 everywhere else.

2. Keytar Hero

Is there a cuter instrument than the keytar? If there is I don’t want to know about it. Half-keyboard, half guitar, all-awesomeness. Show your loved one that they’re the keytar hero in your life. £3.40 shipped to the UK, from £4.65 elsewhere.

3. Yeah, Doge

Doge’s meme moment may have passed, but the Shiba Inu may never be surpassed in puppy-dog cuteness levels. Ships to the UK for £5.32 from the US, but if you miss the shipping deadline there’s also a digital version available. And in the Tiny Bee Etsy shop you’ll find many more examples of such romance.

4. Dino-mite Pun-work

Sometimes you need to say it with dinosaurs. Adorable, roar-some little line-drawing dinosaurs with a little love heart stuck to their bums. This is the card for that. £4.20 shipped to the UK.

5. Sugar For Your Honey

Let your valentine know you think they’re sweeter than a whole bag of sugar. £2.50 shipped to the UK.
