5 Humorous Cards For Your Funny Valentine

Our pick of the funnies.

1. Long Live The Kling

Ben Kling is pretty much the king when it comes to funny valentines. He's got a lot of the bases covered; Harry Potter, Comedians, Favourite Dictators... Leon Trotsky would get our vote if we had one.

2. Breathless

Let your loved one know that they deliver a medicated shot of pressurised love directly to your lungs whenever you see them, with the iconic ventolin inhaler.

3. Do it Like They Do On The Discovery Channel

Perfect for the lover that can't stop showing you their bony frill, this pair of horny creatures.

4. Never Let Them Go

Let them know that you'll never let them go, and your pieces will be forever interlocked in an eternal embrace.

5. Netflix and Chilli

If you think your partner is off the Scoville scale in terms of hotness, let them know your love for them will never go chilli.
